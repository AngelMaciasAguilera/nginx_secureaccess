The Head Chef · Alejandro Romero

With decades of expertise in Japanese haute cuisine, our chef turns simple ingredients into gastronomic art. Trained in Kyoto and Tokyo's finest kitchens, he blends tradition with boundless creativity.

Renowned for his precision and innovation, he leads every aspect of our Michelin-starred experience, setting the standard for excellence.
