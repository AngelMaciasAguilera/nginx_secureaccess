Menu Senshi

pvp: 450€

Appetizers

Hikari no Hada: Dashi sphere with yuzu essence and shiso leaf · Imperial Oyster: Kumamoto oyster with ponzu pearls and oscietra caviar · Kaisen no Hana: Tempura flower stuffed with snow crab meat and fresh wasabi.

Starters

Sakura Sashimi: Premium sashimi selection (otoro, hamachi, uni, and akami) served over cherry blossom-infused ice · Wagyu Tartare: A5 Wagyu tartare with white soy emulsion and black truffle · Ebi Tempura: Tiger prawn tempura with matcha salt · Truffled Chawanmushi: Japanese egg custard with dashi broth and black truffle slices.

Interlude

Rodaballo Yaki: Grilled turbot glazed with white miso · Smoked Eel: Kabayaki eel with jasmine rice and fresh sprouts · Binchotan Wagyu: A5 Wagyu grilled over binchotan charcoal with umeboshi plum sauce · Duck Tsukune: Japanese-style duck meatball glazed with tare sauce and cured egg yolk · Yuzu Granité: Yuzu snow with candied ginger and sake powder.

Desserts

Zen Matcha: Green tea mousse with black sesame crumble · Sakura Ice Cream: Cherry blossom ice cream with sweet sake jelly · Artisanal Mochi: Flavors of kinako, black sesame, and strawberry · White Chocolate Bonbon: Filled with sake.

Drinks

Complete pairing includes: Junmai Daiginjo sake · Light Riesling and Chardonnay wines · Artisanal Umeshu digestif.
