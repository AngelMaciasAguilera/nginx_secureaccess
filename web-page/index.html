Founded in 1978 by Kaito Nishida

How it all started

Our journey began with a deep admiration for Japanese culinary tradition and a desire to bring its essence to life. Inspired by the artistry and precision of Japanese cuisine, we envisioned a place where every dish tells a story.

From humble beginnings to Michelin acclaim, our passion drove us to create an experience that honors Japanese culture. Each element, from the menu to the ambiance, is crafted to immerse guests in a true taste of Japan.
